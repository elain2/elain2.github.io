## Responsibilities

- Joined Yuki camera/graphics framework team supporting LINE's media products
- Owned CI/CD pipeline and Jenkins-based framework deployment to internal CocoaPods
- Debugged complex Objective-C runtime issues across framework consumers
- Maintained backward compatibility for Swift and Objective-C interoperability
- Collaborated with multiple product teams (Avatar, Voom, Messenger) on framework integration

## About

Yuki is LINE's internal camera and graphics framework used across multiple products. Deep experience with framework-level development, Objective-C runtime debugging, and CI/CD automation. This role built strong foundations for later leading product teams that consumed the framework.
